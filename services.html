Our Services

We offer a range of services that cater to your talent acquisition needs, including:

Consulting Services

Our Consulting Services Division offers tailored business solutions to address your specific needs. We provide a range of options, including contingent - no cure no pay, retained - part payment in stages, contained - contingent first, then final stages retained, and retingent - retained first, then final stages contingent.

With our expertise in talent acquisition and recruitment, we can help you navigate the complex landscape of finding and hiring the right people for your organization. Our team of experienced consultants can provide guidance on sourcing strategies, candidate screening and selection, and negotiating compensation packages. Whether you need short-term support or long-term solutions, we can work with you to develop a customized plan that meets your unique requirements. We pride ourselves on delivering results that exceed expectations, and we are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients.

Project Outsourcing

Project Outsourcing is a service we offer to help our clients fill talent gaps on a temporary basis. We understand the importance of having the right talent at the right time, and we are here to provide you with high-quality contract recruitment services. Our team of experts can help you find and hire contract-based professionals within 24-72 hours, whether you need them hired on our payroll or directly on yours.

We also offer a Temp-to-Perm option, where you can hire on our payroll first and then convert the employee when approved. With our access to high-end industry-leading contract-based professionals, you can trust that we will provide you with the best talent for your project needs.

Staff Augmentation

One of the biggest challenges for any talent acquisition team is to save recruitment cost. Companies prefer to set up their internal talent team with a notion of saving cost. They spend money on permanent recruiters, job boards, networking portals, events etc. They don’t prefer going to external recruitment partners as due to higher fees structures.

There are times when the internal talent acquisition teams are required to react to the business needs but fail to have capacity and resources to deliver – specifically hiring due to an urgent project, spikes in demand or other circumstances that lead to a resource shortage. The business may not have the funding to use external recruitment firms, nor the time to take on an RPO process, or expand the internal talent resourcing team, as this typically requires a large amount of effort in all cases. The client simply needs short term specialist help and security that they can handle the workload without dramatically increasing the cost to hire / time to hire.
